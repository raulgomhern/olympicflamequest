We are in Olympia in front of the Temple of Hera.
The priestesses are singing odes to the Goddess.
The Olympic Games are about to start and they are praying for her protection.
Later on, the OLYMPIC FLAME will be lit with the fire from the cauldron.

This is a symbol of purity, perfection and life.

In this process citizens from all over the World will be joined by the Olympic Spirit.
